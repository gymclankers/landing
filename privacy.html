Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

GymClankers ("we", "us") is an AI music project. This policy explains what data we collect when you visit gymclankers.com.

What we collect

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors find and use the site. This collects:

  • Pages visited and time on site
  • Referring site or search engine
  • Browser type, device type, screen size
  • Approximate geographic region (country/city level)

Google Analytics uses cookies. You can opt out by disabling cookies in your browser or using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

What we don't collect

We do not collect names, email addresses, payment information, or any personally identifiable information. There are no accounts, sign-ups, or forms on this site.

Third-party platforms

The site links to Spotify, TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, Twitter/X, Discord, and Telegram. When you visit those platforms, their own privacy policies apply.

Audio streaming

Song previews are served from Cloudflare R2. No personal data is collected during playback.

Contact

Questions about this policy: reach us on any of our social channels or via Discord.
